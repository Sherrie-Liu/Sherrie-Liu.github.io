Environmental Modeler

Using modeling to investigate the relationship and influence between various environmental elements.

My current research centers on top-down carbon cycle studies over the Amazon, using satellite observations (OCO-2/OCO-3) and atmospheric inverse modeling to quantify regional carbon fluxes and investigate ecosystem responses to climate variability, including the impacts of recent El Niño events. My previous research focused on hydrological and water environmental modeling, with particular interests in watershed systems and soil water transfer processes.
